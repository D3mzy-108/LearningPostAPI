Learning Quests Tailored to You

These aren't one-size-fits-all quizzes. Quests are carefully crafted to align with each student's unique strengths and needs. Areas of weakness are targeted, while concepts already mastered are skipped over - eliminating wasted time. Every quest is an personalized, efficient path to conquering knowledge gaps.

Interactive Multimodal Adventures

Static learning is a relic. Our multmodal approach to learning integrates audio-visual stimuli in order to expand the knowledge horizon, on demand. Best of all, it's a two-way street: interactive features let students contribute to the direction of their learning journeys based on personal needs, preferences and styles.

Gamified Learning Pathways

The gaming fun doesn't stop at points and badges. Learners can take the spotlight in live "Challenge" quizzes, going head-to-head with peers in a celebration of capabilities. These real-time contests allow students to showcase their knowledge mastery for recognition and prizes.

Flexible Bite-Sized, Self-Paced Learning

Knowledge is served in digestible, on-demand portions that fit your schedule. With 24/7 access to resources and support, learners enjoy ultimate flexibility to tailor study sessions to their personal styles, needs and pace. Small doses of learning, massive long-term gains.

Mastery Learning: No Learner Left Behind

LearningPost believes fundamentals matter more than skipping ahead. That's why comprehensive mastery of core concepts is prioritized over rushing to advanced topics. Review and remediation are actively encouraged until each student has solidified the essential foundations to level up confidently, with no learner left behind.
